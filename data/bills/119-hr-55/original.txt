R. 55 To repeal the National Voter Registration Act of 1993. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 3, 2025 Mr. Biggs of Arizona (for himself and Mr. Perry) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on House Administration _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To repeal the National Voter Registration Act of 1993. The National Voter Registration Act of 1993 (52 U.S.C. 20501 et seq.) is repealed. &lt;all&gt;
