[Congressional Bills 119th Congress] [From the U.S. Government Publishing Office] [S. 203 Introduced in Senate (IS)] &lt;DOC&gt; 119th CONGRESS 1st Session S. 203 To prohibit Federal funding of Planned Parenthood Federation of America. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES January 23, 2025 Mr. Paul (for himself, Mr. Marshall, Mr. Tuberville, Mr. Mullin, Mr. Budd, Mr. Schmitt, Mr. Tillis, Mr. Cruz, and Mr. Banks) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Health, Education, Labor, and Pensions _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To prohibit Federal funding of Planned Parenthood Federation of America. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Defund Planned Parenthood Act''. SEC. 2. PROHIBITION. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be made available to Planned Parenthood Federation of America, or to any of its affiliates. &lt;all&gt;
