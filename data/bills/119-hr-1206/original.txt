R. 1206 To require the Director of the Bureau of Land Management to withdraw a rule of the Bureau of Land Management relating to conservation and landscape health. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES February 11, 2025 Ms. Maloy (for herself, Mr. Fulcher, Ms. Hageman, Mr. Newhouse, Mr. Owens, Mr. Bentz, and Mr. Zinke) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To require the Director of the Bureau of Land Management to withdraw a rule of the Bureau of Land Management relating to conservation and landscape health. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Western Economic Security Today Act of 2025'' or the ``WEST Act of 2025''. SEC. 2. WITHDRAWAL OF BLM RULE. The final rule based on the proposed rule of the Bureau of Land Management entitled ``Conservation and Landscape Health'' (88 Fed. Reg. 19583 (April 3, 2023)) shall have no force or effect. &lt;all&gt;
