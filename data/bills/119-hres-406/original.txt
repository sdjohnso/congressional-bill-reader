RES. 406 Removing certain Members from certain standing committees of the House of Representatives. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES May 13, 2025 Mr. Carter of Georgia submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Ethics _______________________________________________________________________ RESOLUTION Removing certain Members from certain standing committees of the House of Representatives. Resolved, That the following named Members be, and are hereby, removed from the following standing committees of the House of Representatives: Committee on Appropriations: Mrs. Watson Coleman. Committee on Energy and Commerce: Mr. Menendez. Committee on Homeland Security: Mrs. McIver. Committee on Small Business: Mrs. McIver. &lt;all&gt;
