RES. 10 Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to establish a Committee on Health as a standing committee of the House. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 3, 2025 Mr. Davidson submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Rules _______________________________________________________________________ RESOLUTION Amending the Rules of the House of Representatives to establish a Committee on Health as a standing committee of the House. Resolved, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This resolution may be cited as the ``House Endeavor to Accelerate a Legislative Transformation of Healthcare Act'' or the ``HEALTH Act''. SEC. 2. ESTABLISHMENT OF COMMITTEE ON HEALTH. (a) In General.--Clause 1 of rule X of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended-- (1) by redesignating paragraphs (j) through (t) as paragraphs (k) through (u); and (2) by inserting after paragraph (i) the following new paragraph: ``(j) Committee on health.-- ``(A) Biomedical research and development, including the Food and Drug Administration. ``(B) Health, health facilities, and health care supported by general revenues (except veterans' hospitals, medical care, and treatment of veterans). ``(C) Public health and quarantine, including the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.''. (b) Conforming Amendments to Existing Jurisdiction of Standing Committees.-- (1) Committee on education and the workforce.--Clause 1(e)(6) of such rule is amended by striking ``generally'' and inserting ``generally (except health insurance programs)''. (2) Committee on energy and commerce.--Clause 1(f) of such rule is amended-- (A) by striking subparagraphs (1), (3), and (12); and (B) by redesignating the remaining subparagraphs accordingly. &lt;all&gt;
