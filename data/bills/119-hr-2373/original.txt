R. 2373 To amend the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022 with respect to a civil action relating to the disclosure of intimate images. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES March 26, 2025 Ms. Mace introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To amend the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022 with respect to a civil action relating to the disclosure of intimate images. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Increased Accountability for Nonconsensual Pornography Act of 2025''. SEC. 2. CIVIL ACTION RELATING TO DISCLOSURE OF INTIMATE IMAGES. Section 1309 of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022 (15 U.S.C. 6851) is amended-- (1) in subsection (a)-- (A) in paragraph (2), by inserting ``competent,'' after ``conscious,''; and (B) in paragraph (5)(A)-- (i) in clause (i), by striking ``or'' at the end; (ii) in clause (ii)-- (I) in subclause (I), by striking the semicolon at the end and inserting ``; or''; (II) in subclause (II), by striking ``; or'' and inserting a semicolon; and (III) by striking subclause (III); and (iii) by adding at the end the following: ``(iii) an identifiable individual engaging in sexually explicit conduct; and''; and (2) in subsection (b)(3)(A)(i), by striking ``$150,000'' and inserting ``$500,000''. &lt;all&gt;
