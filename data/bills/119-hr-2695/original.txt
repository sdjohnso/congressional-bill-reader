R. 2695 To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize grants for building communities of recovery. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES April 7, 2025 Ms. Pettersen (for herself and Mr. Nunn of Iowa) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize grants for building communities of recovery. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Communities of Recovery Reauthorization Act of 2025''. SEC. 2. BUILDING COMMUNITIES OF RECOVERY. Section 547(f) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 290ee- 2(f)) is amended by striking ``$5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023'' and inserting ``$17,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030''. &lt;all&gt;
