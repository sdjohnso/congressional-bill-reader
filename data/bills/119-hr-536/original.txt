R. 536 To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the energy credit for qualified biogas property. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 16, 2025 Ms. Scholten (for herself and Mr. Valadao) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the energy credit for qualified biogas property. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Agricultural Environmental Stewardship Act of 2025''. SEC. 2. EXTENSION OF ENERGY CREDIT FOR QUALIFIED BIOGAS PROPERTY. (a) In General.--Section 48(c)(7)(C) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking ``December 31, 2024'' and inserting ``December 31, 2025''. (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by this section shall apply to property the construction of which begins after December 31, 2024. &lt;all&gt;
