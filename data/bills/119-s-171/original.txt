[Congressional Bills 119th Congress] [From the U.S. Government Publishing Office] [S. 171 Introduced in Senate (IS)] &lt;DOC&gt; 119th CONGRESS 1st Session S. 171 To remove the lesser prairie-chicken from the lists of threatened species and endangered species published pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, to amend that Act to exclude the lesser prairie- chicken from the authority of that Act, and for other purposes. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES January 21, 2025 Mr. Marshall (for himself, Mr. Lankford, Mr. Mullin, and Mr. Moran) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Environment and Public Works _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To remove the lesser prairie-chicken from the lists of threatened species and endangered species published pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, to amend that Act to exclude the lesser prairie- chicken from the authority of that Act, and for other purposes. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. DELISTING OF LESSER PRAIRIE-CHICKEN. (a) In General.--Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the lesser prairie-chicken (Tympanuchus pallidicinctus), including each distinct population segment of the lesser prairie-chicken (Tympanuchus pallidicinctus), is removed from the lists of threatened species and endangered species that are published pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq.). (b) Exclusion of Lesser Prairie-Chicken From Listing.--Section 4(a) of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (16 U.S.C. 1533(a)) is amended-- (1) in paragraph (1), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking ``The Secretary shall'' and inserting ``Except as provided in paragraph (4), the Secretary shall''; and (2) by adding at the end the following: ``(4) Applicability to Lesser Prairie-chicken.--The Secretary may not make a determination under this subsection that the lesser prairie- chicken (Tympanuchus pallidicinctus) is an endangered species or a threatened species.''. &lt;all&gt;
