R. 2863 To prohibit the use of any United States weapon, weapons system, munition, aircraft, vessel, boat, or other implement of war against Christian properties and Christian civilians in any foreign country. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES April 10, 2025 Mrs. Luna (for herself and Mr. Burchett) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To prohibit the use of any United States weapon, weapons system, munition, aircraft, vessel, boat, or other implement of war against Christian properties and Christian civilians in any foreign country. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Civilians Resisting Unlawful Strikes Against Divine Establishments Act'' or ``CRUSADE Act''. SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON USE OF ANY UNITED STATES WEAPON, WEAPONS SYSTEM, MUNITION, AIRCRAFT, VESSEL, BOAT, OR OTHER IMPLEMENT OF WAR AGAINST ANY CHRISTIAN PROPERTIES AND CHRISTIAN CIVILIANS IN FOREIGN COUNTRIES. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the President shall take such actions as may be necessary to ensure that any weapon, weapons system, munition, aircraft, vessel, boat, or other implement of war provided by the United States to any foreign country may not be used against any of the following: (1) Christian properties in any foreign country, including churches or other religious buildings, sites, or monuments, and real or personal property of any Christian persons. (2) Christian civilians in any foreign country. &lt;all&gt;
