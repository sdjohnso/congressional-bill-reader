R. 231 To amend the Energy and Water Development and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2015, to reauthorize the Colorado River System conservation pilot program. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 7, 2025 Ms. Hageman introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To amend the Energy and Water Development and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2015, to reauthorize the Colorado River System conservation pilot program. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Colorado River Basin System Conservation Extension Act of 2025''. SEC. 2. REAUTHORIZATION OF COLORADO RIVER SYSTEM CONSERVATION PILOT PROGRAM. Section 206 of the Energy and Water Development and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2015 (43 U.S.C. 620 note; Public Law 113- 235), is amended-- (1) in subsection (b)(2), by striking ``this Act'' and inserting ``the Colorado River Basin System Conservation Extension Act of 2025''; (2) in subsection (c)(2), by striking ``2024'' and inserting ``2026''; and (3) in subsection (d), by striking ``2025'' and inserting ``2027''. &lt;all&gt;
