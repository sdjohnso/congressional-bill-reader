R. 522 To ensure equal treatment for certain faith-based organizations in certain Small Business Administration programs. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 16, 2025 Mr. Mann (for himself, Mr. Schmidt, and Mr. Babin) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Small Business _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To ensure equal treatment for certain faith-based organizations in certain Small Business Administration programs. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Fair Assistance and Impartial Treatment of Help In Small Business Act'' or the ``FAITH in Small Business Act''. SEC. 2. ENSURING EQUAL TREATMENT FOR CERTAIN FAITH-BASED ORGANIZATIONS IN CERTAIN SMALL BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION PROGRAMS. The proposed rule of the Small Business Administration titled ``Ensuring Equal Treatment for Faith-Based Organizations in SBA's Loan and Disaster Assistance Programs'' issued January 19, 2021, shall have the force and effect of law. &lt;all&gt;
