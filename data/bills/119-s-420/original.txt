[Congressional Bills 119th Congress] [From the U.S. Government Publishing Office] [S. 420 Introduced in Senate (IS)] &lt;DOC&gt; 119th CONGRESS 1st Session S. 420 To amend the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 to reauthorize the dairy business innovation initiatives. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES February 5, 2025 Ms. Baldwin (for herself and Mrs. Blackburn) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To amend the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 to reauthorize the dairy business innovation initiatives. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Dairy Business Innovation Act of 2025''. SEC. 2. DAIRY BUSINESS INNOVATION INITIATIVES. Section 12513(i) of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (7 U.S.C. 1632d(i)) is amended by striking ``$20,000,000'' and inserting ``$36,000,000''. &lt;all&gt;
