R. 691 To designate the Washington Dulles International Airport in Virginia as the ``Donald J. Trump International Airport''. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 23, 2025 Mr. McDowell (for himself, Mr. Reschenthaler, Mr. Gill of Texas, Mr. Moore of West Virginia, and Mr. Jack) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To designate the Washington Dulles International Airport in Virginia as the ``Donald J. Trump International Airport''. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. DESIGNATION OF DONALD J. TRUMP INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT. (a) Designation.--The Washington Dulles International Airport in Virginia shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the ``Donald J. Trump International Airport''. (b) Reference.--Any reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the airport referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Donald J. Trump International Airport. &lt;all&gt;
