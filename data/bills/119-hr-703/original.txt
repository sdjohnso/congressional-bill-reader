_______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make permanent the deduction for qualified business income. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Main Street Tax Certainty Act''. SEC. 2. DEDUCTION FOR QUALIFIED BUSINESS INCOME MADE PERMANENT. (a) In General.--Section 199A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subsection (i). (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025. &lt;all&gt;
