RES. 26 Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Heavy-Duty Vehicles--Phase 3''. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 22, 2025 Mr. Fulcher submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce _______________________________________________________________________ JOINT RESOLUTION Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Heavy-Duty Vehicles--Phase 3''. Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Heavy-Duty Vehicles--Phase 3'' (89 Fed. Reg. 29440 (April 22, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect. &lt;all&gt;
