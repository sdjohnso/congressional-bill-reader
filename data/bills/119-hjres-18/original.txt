RES. 18 Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``National Primary Drinking Water Regulations for Lead and Copper: Improvements (LCRI)''. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 13, 2025 Mr. Palmer submitted the following joint resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and Commerce _______________________________________________________________________ JOINT RESOLUTION Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``National Primary Drinking Water Regulations for Lead and Copper: Improvements (LCRI)''. Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to ``National Primary Drinking Water Regulations for Lead and Copper: Improvements (LCRI)'' (89 Fed. Reg. 86418 (October 30, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect. &lt;all&gt;
