R. 2112 To give the force and effect of law to the Executive Order issued on March 6, 2025 entitled ``Establishment of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and United States Digital Asset Stockpile''. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES March 14, 2025 Mr. Donalds introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Financial Services _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To give the force and effect of law to the Executive Order issued on March 6, 2025 entitled ``Establishment of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and United States Digital Asset Stockpile''. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. ESTABLISHMENT OF THE STRATEGIC BITCOIN RESERVE AND UNITED STATES DIGITAL ASSET STOCKPILE. The Executive order issued on March 6, 2025 entitled ``Establishment of the Strategic Bitcoin Reserve and United States Digital Asset Stockpile'' shall have the force and effect on law. &lt;all&gt;
