R. 408 To nullify the Presidential memoranda on the withdrawal of certain areas of the outer Continental Shelf from oil or natural gas leasing. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES January 15, 2025 Mr. Arrington introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Natural Resources _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To nullify the Presidential memoranda on the withdrawal of certain areas of the outer Continental Shelf from oil or natural gas leasing. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. NULLIFICATION OF PRESIDENTIAL MEMORANDA ON THE WITHDRAWAL OF CERTAIN AREAS OF THE OUTER CONTINENTAL SHELF. The following Presidential memoranda shall have no force or effect: (1) The Presidential Memorandum of January 6, 2025, titled ``Memorandum on the Withdrawal of Certain Areas of the United States Outer Continental Shelf from Oil or Natural Gas Leasing'' (relating to the Gulf of Mexico, Atlantic, and Pacific areas). (2) The Presidential Memorandum of January 6, 2025, titled ``Memorandum on the Withdrawal of Certain Areas of the United States Outer Continental Shelf from Oil or Natural Gas Leasing'' (relating to the Northern Bering Sea Climate Resilience Area). &lt;all&gt;
