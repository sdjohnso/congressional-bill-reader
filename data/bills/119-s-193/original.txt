[Congressional Bills 119th Congress] [From the U.S. Government Publishing Office] [S. 193 Introduced in Senate (IS)] &lt;DOC&gt; 119th CONGRESS 1st Session S. 193 To repeal the Alien Enemies Act. _______________________________________________________________________ IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES January 22, 2025 Ms. Hirono (for herself, Mr. Booker, Ms. Duckworth, Mr. Durbin, Mr. Markey, Mr. Sanders, and Ms. Warren) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on the Judiciary _______________________________________________________________________ A BILL To repeal the Alien Enemies Act. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE. This Act may be cited as the ``Neighbors Not Enemies Act''. SEC. 2. REPEAL OF ALIEN ENEMIES ACT. Sections 4067 through 4070 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (50 U.S.C. 21-24) are repealed. &lt;all&gt;
